Dr. Vivek Murthy, U.S. Surgeon General opened the event with remarks and introduced the Bice President.

Vice President Harris called the surgeon general “tireless” in his efforts to combat COVID-19.

The Vice President highlighted faith leaders, union leaders and business leaders were in attendance and part of the founding members coalition.

A “Share the Mic” program was mentioned as an option for members to host a Q&A session with a doctor or nurse on the member’s social media platforms.

“The vaccine is safe and it will save lives,” is one point Vice President Harris emphasis in her remarks.
