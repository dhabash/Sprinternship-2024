Good morning from the White House, where it is a humid 62 degrees with some sun and looming gray skies.

The pool is getting covid tested and gathering at 9:50am for an anticipated 10:15am departure. NOTE: this is an hour earlier than planned to beat the weather.

This 3-day trip (Weds 4/21 to Fri 4/23) to Albuquerque, New Mexico, and Navajo Nation in Window Rock and St. Michael's, Arizona, will be Dr. Biden's longest solo trip as First Lady.

The previous trip to California (the one over April Fool's where she pranked the pool by passing out Dove Bars disguised as a flight attendant) was the second-longest at two days.

The trip will be to highlight vaccine efforts. FLOTUS will be visiting a vaccine center in ABQ today, 4/21, will deliver a radio address to the Navajo Nation on 4/22, and will hold a listening session with students from Navajo Nation and visit a vaccination site on 4/23. Returning to the White House 4/23 in the evening.

Your pooler was born and raised in New Mexico and is very excited to go home, even for just a day.

Here is the schedule the First Lady's office sent out yesterday. We have not yet been advised as to how the early departure will affect the schedule.
