Motorcade was accompanied by a fleet of what looked like 15-20 police on motorcycles. Zipping down University Blvd by the airport, I-25 and Avenida Cesar Chavez. Passed a mural with native imagery, a restaurant called Taqueria El Dorado, a Sonic Drive-in, HealingWaters Ministry, Iglesias Dios Pentecostal, Norteñitos Mexican food, signs about how to qualify for pre-k, a place where you can get a vaccine with a marquee saying you can get a grab & go meal, muffler shops, tire shops. This area of ABQ is flat with a mixture of flat roofed adobe houses and pitched roof houses, mostly one story tall.

Crossed a bridge over the Rio Grande.

People at Pedro Baca Head Start stood outside filming with cell phones and waving at motorcade. Diverse crowd.

2:09pm arrival at vaccine center (or health center administering vaccinations), First Choice Community Healthcare, South Valley Medical Center. It is in the South Valley neighborhood.

FLOTUS entered the building before we did.

The only tarmac greeter to accompany FLOTUS to the site is Gov. Michelle Lujan Grisham. They will be shown around by Dr. Will Kaufman, medical director of FCCH.

First Choice Community Healthcare is the name of a nonprofit that runs nine health centers in three counties in central New Mexico, one of which is school-based. They’re participating in a federal program called Health Center COVID-19 Program, and are issuing the Moderna vaccine.

BACKGROUND FROM THE FIRST LADY’S OFFICE

The First Lady will visit First Choice Community Healthcare, South Valley Medical Center, a nonprofit federally qualified health center in Albuquerque, New Mexico, that has been administering COVID-19 vaccinations. She will highlight the critical work of COVID-19 vaccination centers and the Administration’s efforts to ensure that all Americans can get vaccinated. The First Lady and New Mexico Governor Michelle Lujan Grisham will receive a briefing on the vaccination center, including their experience over the past few months.

First Choice Community Healthcare currently provides community-based healthcare at nine locations (eight health centers and one school-based health center) in three counties in central New Mexico. They offer medical, dental, and Women, Infants, and Children (WIC) program services, including at their South Valley Medical Center, which FLOTUS will visit. First Choice Community Healthcare serves over 50,000 unique patients annually.

First Choice Community Healthcare offers COVID-19 vaccines at each of their locations, including the South Valley Medical Center that FLOTUS will visit.

First Choice is currently administering Moderna vaccines. First Choice is also participating in the Health Center COVID-19 Vaccine Program, which is a federal program.

HEALTH CENTER GREETER, BRIEFER, AND TOUR GUIDE

Dr. Will Kaufman is the Medical Director of First Choice Community Healthcare, a federally qualified community health center system operating eight clinics and one school-based health care center in three counties in central New Mexico. Dr. Kaufman practices Family Medicine at the First Choice clinic located within the South Valley Health Commons, a model of integrated medical, behavioral, dental, public health, and WIC services in the historic South Valley of Bernalillo County. Dr. Kaufman completed Medical School at the University of New Mexico School of Medicine, Family Medicine Residency at The Lawrence Family Practice Residency, and a Master’s in Public Health at the Harvard School of Public Health.
