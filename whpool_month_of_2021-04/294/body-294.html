Good morning,

I will be your print pooler for the vice-president today.

Vice-President Kamala Harris is due to meet Japanese prime minister Yoshihide Suga at 11 am in the ceremonial office in the Eisenhower Executive Office building. There will be a short pool spray at the beginning of the meeting.


Some background:

Yoshihide Suga became Japan’s Prime Minister in September last year, succeeding Shinzo Abe who retired due to ill health.

This is the first visit of a foreign leader to the White House of the Biden presidency, though PM Suga did participate in a virtual meeting of the “Quad” last month with President Biden, alongside the leaders of India, Australia.

Mr Suga arrived last night at Joint Base Andrews and stayed overnight at Blair House.

Following his bilateral meeting with the vice-president, he will meet with President Biden at 1.30pm in the Oval Office, followed by an expanded meeting in the State Dining Room at 2.30 pm. The two leaders will hold a press conference at 4.15 pm.
